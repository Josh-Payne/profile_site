Projects

  • Albums

    Josh has collaborated with and and continues to work with several talented musicians to create the albums shown. On Impulse, Season of Silver, and the soon-to-be-released Deconstruction, the instrumentation includes Mario Dominguez on tenor sax, Brendan Frederick on alto sax, Adam Carrillo on keyboards and saxes, Ryan Whetten on guitars, AJ Magallan on basses, and Josh on drums, congas, and other percussion.
    Josh's other roles on these albums include primary composer, sound engineer, and publisher. They were primarily recorded and engineered at Josh's house and are mixed and mastered by Josh using Logic Pro X.

  • On Jazz and Civil Rights

  • Written Composition

    An enthusiastic composer of both music and literature, Josh has written articles and press releases for The Helotes Echo, Helotes Highlights, and Modern Jazz Today. He has given interviews with KTSA 107.1 FM and KRTU 91.7 FM and his music has been played on many radio stations and online streaming websites (details on the about page). He also recently composed a brief treatise regarding the interplay between jazz and the Civil Rights Movement of the 1950s and 60s, linked above.
